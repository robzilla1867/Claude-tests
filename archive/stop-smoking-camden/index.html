Ways to stop smoking

Pick the path that fits your life

There's no single "right way" to quit — most people combine two or three of these. Our advisors will help you build a personal plan, free of charge.

Nicotine replacement therapy

Patches, gum, lozenges and sprays available free through our service to ease cravings while you break the habit.

Learn more →

Stop-smoking medication

Prescription options like varenicline or bupropion, discussed with a clinician to see what suits your health needs.

Learn more →

Vaping as a step-down

E-cigarettes can help long-term smokers cut down safely — our advisors can talk you through starter kits and local offers.

Learn more →

Free 1‑to‑1 coaching

Weekly check-ins with a trained stop-smoking advisor — in person at your GP surgery, over the phone, or by video call.

Find a service →

Quit-tracking apps

Track cravings, savings and smoke-free days with NHS-approved apps that send you a boost right when you need it.

Learn more →

Community support groups

Weekly drop-in groups across Camden where you can share the journey with people quitting at the same time as you.
