About Aurelia

Where clinical expertise meets quiet elegance

Founded on the belief that the best aesthetic work is the kind no one notices, Aurelia Aesthetics brings together GMC-registered practitioners, medical-grade technology and an unhurried approach to consultation.

Our clinic in Mayfair was designed as a sanctuary — soft light, considered materials, and a team who listen before they treat. We believe in enhancing what is already there, never replacing it.
