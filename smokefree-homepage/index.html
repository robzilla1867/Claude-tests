What you'll get

Every treatment you need, completely free

Stopping smoking brings health benefits from the moment you quit. We remove the cost barrier so you can focus on what matters — staying smokefree for good.

  • Free nicotine replacement therapy (NRT), including patches and gum
  • Free vapes and e-liquids to support your quit
  • Ongoing one-to-one guidance from trained stop smoking advisors
  • Flexible in-person or telephone appointments near you
