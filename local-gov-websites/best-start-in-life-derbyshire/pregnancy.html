Finding out you're pregnant can bring a lot of questions. This section brings together the practical information and support available to you in Derbyshire, from registering your pregnancy through to preparing for your baby's arrival.

If you're not sure where to start, that's OK — take a look at the topics below, or speak to your midwifery team about anything you're unsure of.

Register your pregnancy Let your midwifery team know as early as you can, so we can start planning your care with you. Antenatal care and appointments What to expect from your antenatal appointments, scans and checks throughout your pregnancy. Healthy Start vouchers Check if you're eligible for help buying healthy food, milk and vitamins during your pregnancy. Preparing for birth Information on birth choices, birth plans and what to pack for hospital or a home birth. Mental health and wellbeing Support if you're feeling anxious, low or overwhelmed during pregnancy — you're not alone.
